    I AM Tahmid Sahriar

    also known as Sherlock Holmes .



    a versatile photographer specializing in portraits, events, fashion, entertainment, and documentary photography. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for capturing life's moments, I create images that tell compelling stories. My work is a fusion of creativity, skill, and vision, bringing out the best in my subjects.



    We love to tell what we see, understand, we got from our existence on this earth. Like every human being, I have cravings for storytelling. People tell their stories through writing, painting, songs, dance, movie making, and many other ways. One of those ways that I took is photography. I want to tell stories through photographs.

    Moreover, I intend to have time with various types of people. As a good listener, I'm passionate to listen to people's stories. Being a photographer I always look for stories. Most of the time I love to capture the happiness on people's faces. Colors make me inspired. Also being a team-mate makes me intend to make something of a new perspective.
